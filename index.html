Happiness Program

Rediscovering pregnancy care. Naturally

Pregyanam’s Happiness program is intended to beautify and solidify the relationship you have with your partner. Available across the spectrum of pregnancy as well as before and after, it aims to empower the couple, by focusing on not just one partner but both equally. It addresses issues that are relevant to a modern setting where professional choices can often conflict with personal preferences. It further places the onus on both the partners by providing them clear concise plans and outlining the benefits that are inherent in a loving relationship.


What does “post-partum mental care” mean?

After childbirth, a lot of women are subject to a form of depression which occurs because of childbirth. “Post-partum depression” can be channelized into positive energy by being aware and having the right persons to discuss it with. A Pregyanam mentor is aware of this aspect and provides guidance on how to manage such an issue were it to arise using different mental and emotional exercises.

Who is a Pregyanam mentor and what is a mentoring session?

A Pregyanam mentor for the purposes of this program is a qualified relationship counsellor whose interaction with you and your partner during a mentoring session will help in generating the right perspective towards the relationship dynamic that exits. They are there to listen, to answer your doubts, to advise and ultimately to empower you with the confidence that you deserve for a healthy & happy pregnancy experience.

How does a relationship counsellor help?

Every couple needs an extra nudge to beautify their connect with each other, even more so around and during the period of pregnancy. A certified relationship counselor ensures mental wellness and stability between the couple by managing thoughts & emotions in a positive manner.
